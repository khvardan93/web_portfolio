Բakyi Domino

A culturally authentic Armenian courtyard domino game built around the post-Soviet "Kozel" variant — 4 players in 2v2 pairs, scoring to 101. Custom domino tile renderer with a 3×3 pip grid, beveled tile body, and an Arevakhach medallion on doubles, in an Armenian-themed palette. Targeting WebGL and Facebook Instant Games.
